Global Helplines and Email Support

If you or someone you know is in need of help, you can contact the following global helplines or email support:

Global Helplines:

  • Mental Health America Helpline: 1-800-273-TALK (8255)
  • National Suicide Prevention Lifeline: 1-800-273-8255
  • Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741

Email Support:

  • Mpower: info@mpowerminds.com
  • The Minds Foundation: info@themindsfoundation.org
  • Sangath: sangath@sangath.in

For individuals in India, specific helplines are available for support:

Helplines in India:

  • Vandrevala Foundation Helpline: 1-860-266-2345
  • Aasra Helpline: 91-9820466726
  • Snehi Helpline: 91-9922001122

These organizations provide support through helplines and emails to ensure that help is accessible globally and in India. You are not alone, and there are resources available to support you.
